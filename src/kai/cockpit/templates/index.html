Onboard one agent

From a role you need covered to an agent you supervise.

The job you'd hand a person — not the tool you'd configure. Four steps put an agent on the floor, and you stay in control at every one of them.

  1. 01

    Choose a role

    Pick the seat you'd hire for: support, inbox, or triage.

  2. 02

    Train its Brain

    Give it the briefing you'd give a new hire — documents, your website, notes, the rules you'd repeat out loud.

  3. 03

    Connect work

    Point it at where work already arrives and the systems it's allowed to touch.

  4. 04

    Supervise

    Watch what it handles, correct course, and take it off the floor whenever you want.

Shared knowledge

Train it on what your company knows.

Knowledge tells it what to say. The Brain is shared company knowledge agents consult before company-specific replies — add documents, a website, or notes once, and one Brain feeds every configured agent the relevant context before it decides what to do.
